Craft Video Tutorial

Instructions

  • Cut out a 12.5 cm square of foam
  • Fold the square in half diagonally press down
  • Unfold and fold on the opposite diagonal
  • Make a hold in the centre of the paper (where the lines cross)
  • Cut into each line create but not all the way to the centre
  • Make a hold next to each cut on the diagonal on the left go each side
  • Add a glue dot to each piece not covering the hole
  • Pull in each piece with a hold to meet the hole in the centre and stick it down
  • With excess paper use a glue to indent the paper and cut out circle and make a hole in the centre
  • Repeat previous step twice
  • Wrap pipe cleaner around straw tightly
  • Place one of the disc of paper on the straw and feed the straw through the back of the windmill
  • Place the other disc on the front
  • Using scissors cut the pipe cleaner leaving about a cm
  • Then twist the pipe cleaner into the middle
  • Then blow on the windmill!
